Dr. Gayathri

MBBS, MS (OG), DNB
Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Dr. Gayathri is a consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at KMCH Specialty Hospital, Erode.

She completed her undergraduate MBBS at Government Coimbatore Medical College and pursued her MS in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at Madras Medical College. She has also cleared the Diplomate in the National Board Examination and completed two parts of the MRCOG.

Currently, Dr. Gayathri is pursuing a fellowship in Reproductive Medicine under Dr. Kamini Rao through a hybrid mode.

She gained valuable experience during her two years as a senior resident at Thanjavur Medical College. This training, combined with her advanced expertise, has shaped her into a highly skilled professional in women's health and fertility management.

Known for her kind and compassionate approach, Dr. Gayathri provides personalized care to every patient. She ensures that each treatment plan is tailored to meet the unique needs of her patients, combining clinical precision with empathetic support to deliver outstanding healthcare.

Experience

Dr. Gayathiri has over 10 years of experience in the field of reproductive medicine, having worked at renowned institutions and helped countless patients achieve their health goals. Her dedication to women's health and continuous learning has made her one of the most sought-after specialists in the region.

Our Location

She currently practices in Coimbatore, offering consultations, treatments, and personalized care for women's health. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology to provide the best possible care for our patients.
