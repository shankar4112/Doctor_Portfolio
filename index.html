Dr. Gayathri

MBBS, MS (OG), DNB
Consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Dr. Gayathri is a consultant in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at KMCH Specialty Hospital, Erode.

She completed her undergraduate MBBS at Government Coimbatore Medical College and pursued her MS in Obstetrics & Gynaecology at Madras Medical College. Dr. Gayathri has also cleared the Diplomate in the National Board Examination and successfully completed two parts of the MRCOG. Currently, she is pursuing a fellowship in Reproductive Medicine under Dr. Kamini Rao through a hybrid mode.

With extensive experience in managing normal and caesarean deliveries, as well as handling high-risk pregnancies, Dr. Gayathri's expertise is complemented by her two years as a senior resident at Thanjavur Medical College.

Her compassionate, empathetic, and approachable demeanor makes her highly regarded among patients and colleagues. She is committed to providing personalized care and ensures that each treatment plan is tailored to meet the unique needs of her patients, combining clinical precision with genuine emotional support.

Experience
